1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Plant Images: Photos you take for disease diagnosis are processed on your device and optionally stored in our cloud.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect location to provide region-specific farming recommendations.
  • Community Posts: Content you post in our community forums.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (features used, diagnosis history).
  • Crash Reports: Technical data when the app encounters errors.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered crop disease diagnosis
  • Personalize farming recommendations based on your region and crops
  • Improve our machine learning models (anonymized data only)
  • Send important updates about app features and agricultural alerts
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

  • On-Device Processing: Disease detection runs entirely on your device using on-device ML.
  • Cloud Storage: Your diagnosis history and account data are stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud).
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.
  • Data Retention: We retain your data while your account is active. You can request deletion at any time.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase (authentication, storage), Google Analytics (usage analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Research Partners: Anonymized, aggregated data only, for agricultural research

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your diagnosis history
  • Opt-out of analytics collection

6. Children's Privacy

FieldGuard is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
